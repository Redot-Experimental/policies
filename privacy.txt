At Redot ("we," "our," or "us"), we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal data in connection with our platforms and this website, including its forums.

# 1. Information We Collect

### We collect the following categories of information:
- **Personal Information**: Name, email address, and other contact details you provide when signing up for our forum, services, newsletters, or contacting us. This type of information includes any registration forms you encounter on our website or within the application.
- **Usage Data**: Information related to how you interact with our website, forums and other platforms, including IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and analytical data such as pages viewed and time spent on our website. This information is collected automatically by the analytics tools we use.
- Application Data: Data generated through the use of the Redot application or its plugins, such as version number, IP address, and asset-related searches. 
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**: Information collected via cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze website performance and enhance your experience. See our Cookies Statement below for more details.
- **User Content**: Any feedback, comments, or submissions related to our website, forum, game engine or other services, including text, images, video, voice recordings or any other types of content that may be submitted to us directly or indirectly. This type of data contains everything you create by participating in our forums, chats, or public discussions, as well as data provided through surveys, evaluations, and direct inquiries.

# 11. Cookies Statement

### What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to collect information about your interactions with our website. They help us enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content.

### Types of Cookies We Use:

- **Necessary Cookies**: These are essential for the functioning of our website (e.g., login authentication).
- **Performance Cookies**: These help us understand website usage and improve functionality.
- **Functional Cookies**: These remember your preferences and customize your experience.

### How to Manage Cookies
You can choose to accept or reject non-essential cookies, such as analytics or performance cookies. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For detailed guidance on managing cookies, you can visit <http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies>.
